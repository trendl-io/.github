# Trendl

**We don't follow trends. We compute them.**

Trendl is a data-driven signal service for NQ Futures. We compute trend structure from five years of market data across three validated models.

## Models

- **PULSE** — short-horizon momentum
- **WAVE** — mid-horizon cycles
- **SURGE** — regime breakout

## By the numbers

- 5 years of market data
- 3 validated models
- 88–91% win rate across backtests
- Invitation only
